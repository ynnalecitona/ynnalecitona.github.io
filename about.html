about me

I'm a third year Computer Science major, minoring in Education at UC Davis.

I'm currently the Co-President of Davis Women in Computer Science, hosting weekly events to give our members a platform to advance academically and professionally.

In addition, I am the Co-Vice President of Davis Computer Science Club, and has been part of the organization fro 2 years now.

For a year, I played the Lead Marketing Director of SacHacks, the first intercollegiate hackathon in the Greater Sacramento Area.

I've done plenty of work as a student at UC Davis, and it is all due to the fact that I wanted to give back to my community. Taking on different leadership roles allowed me to learn about what its like working on a group filled with diverse people.

Outside of participating in on campus activities, I like to spend most of my time trying different food and drinks (certified boba lover here!).
