Aircraft Maintenance System

Implemented a program that determines the maintenance schedule of aircraft engines and is able to process different types of aircrafts that requires maintenance at different intervals.

The purpose is to ensure the high level of safety required in air travel.

There are two different types of aircrafts: Airbus A380 (4 engines) and Boeing 737 (2 engines).

Created: Spring 2017
